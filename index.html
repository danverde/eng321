About Me

First thing you need to know about me is that I'm a small dog kind of guy , an ameture photographer, and a car enthusiast. Now on to the boring stuff!

I've studied PHP, mySQL, and Oracle SQL in the past, but they've gotten a bit rusty since I've focused on JavaScript.

I've got a passion for code and for the web. I know how to work hard and I love to learn!
