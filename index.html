About Me

The first thing you need to know about me is that I'm a small dog kind of guy, an amateur photographer, and a car enthusiast. Now on to the boring stuff!

I'm currently a student and a part-time Front-End Web Developer for Brigham Young University-Idaho. When it comes to design, I believe that simple is better. I spend my days maintaing a section of the University homepage, slaving away on homework, and writing custom tools for pretty much any faculty member that asks.

I speak fluent HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Spanish. I also know SASS, NodeJS, and SVG images. I am currently learning Angular4 and Python 2.7. I hope to learn C# and .NET before I graduate in early 2018.

I've studied PHP, mySQL, and Oracle SQL in the past, but they've gotten a bit rusty since I've focused on JavaScript. I'm more than happy to brush up on these skills if necessary.

I've got a passion for code and for the web. I know how to work hard and I love to learn!
